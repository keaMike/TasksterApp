Gather all your tasks in one place!

Are you tired of forgetting what tasks need to be done? Then create all your tasks here and share them with your friends and family! Members of your team can assign themselves tasks, so everybody knows when a task is undergoing. You can also chat to each other in realtime on our chat page.

Invite all your friends and family to help out

Once you have created a team, you are ready to invite all your friends and family! It's easy, you find your team token under your profile link and then all you have to do, is share that token with them! Then they will be able to join a team once they have created a profile!

Enjoy the extra free time

With Taskter you will be able to get a much better overview of what tasks need to be done! Therefore you will have spare time to do what ever you want! So enjoy and relax.
